About Me

I am an engineer, teacher, wife and mom to three children. I have worked in manufacturing, safety and health engineering for 12 years at Texas Instrument, Micro Switch, Tellabs, Tadpole Technology and most recently at Uptime Solutions.

I also have a secondary math certification in Texas. I taught high school amd middle school math for 20 years, 14 of those years teaching 6th grade math at Kealing Middle school.
